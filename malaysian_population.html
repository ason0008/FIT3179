Malaysian Population
In scientific terms, demographics is the study of population based on parameters such as population size, density, age structure, mortality rates, gender ratio and ethnicity ratio. It helps policymakers and world leaders project future population changes and decide if it is sustainable to make informed decisions. Various studies have shown the inverse relationship between country development and country population. This visualisation portrays Malaysia's population index based on a number of factors in a clear and understandable manner.
